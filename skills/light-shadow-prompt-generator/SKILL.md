# Light and Shadow Prompt Generator

Generates cinematic, high-quality image prompts using the "Container + Pressure + Cut + Anchor" aesthetic formula.

## Quick Start

**Input:** A simple theme or empty
**Output:** A detailed, professional image prompt

**Example:**
- Input: "Subway Station"
- Output: "Rough, grime-covered ceramic tiles lining the walls of a desolate underground space. An infinite symmetrical subway tunnel stretching into darkness. 24mm wide angle shot from a low perspective..."

## The Formula (4 Steps)

### 1. Container (The Geometry)
Define the imposing geometric structure.
- *Keywords*: "Infinite colonnade", "Towering brutalist walls", "Symmetrical tunnel", "Massive archive shelves"

### 2. Pressure (The Lens)
Wide-angle lenses and low angles create scale.
- *Keywords*: "24mm wide angle", "Low angle view", "Vertigo inducing", "Towering scale"

### 3. Cut (The Light)
Light as a physical object cutting space.
- *Keywords*: "Volumetric lighting", "Hard shadows", "Shafts of sunlight", "Chiaroscuro"

### 4. Anchor (The Scale)
A single small element for scale.
- *Examples*: "A lone figure in a red dress", "A single chair", "A small campfire"

## Output Format

**Positive Prompt:**
[Material/Atmosphere] + [Container] + [Pressure] + [Cut/Lighting] + [Anchor] + [Details/Props]

**Negative Prompt:**
"flat lighting, low contrast, oversaturated, cartoon, 3d render, watermark, deformed, distorted, ugly"

Ultra-wide panoramic perspective looking up at the canopy. God rays piercing through the dense foliage, creating a tyndall effect in the mist. A single small stone idol placed at the base of a tree. Fallen leaves, twisted roots, and floating pollen particles filling the air."

**Output:**
> "Corroded metal surfaces reflecting neon lights in a narrow back alley. Towering brutalist walls enclosing the space. 24mm wide angle from a low angle, emphasizing the claustrophobic scale. Sharp, cold shafts of neon light cutting through the wet air. A lone figure in a worn jacket standing near a flickering holographic advertisement. Tangled wires, discarded takeout boxes, steam rising from grates. Cyberpunk atmosphere, photorealistic, 8k."

## Tips

- Always start with material and atmosphere
- Make the anchor element small relative to the container
- Include surface imperfections (grime, scratches, wear)
- Use cinematic lighting descriptions, not just "good lighting"
